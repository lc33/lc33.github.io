WHAT IS MENU

Menu is to help you find great local food! We only focus on food. You could find your favourite food by pictures, rates and comments of the food. And furthermore, menu could recommend food based on your personal preference.

Browsing nearby food & restaurants in map view

Once you open menu, you could browse nearby food and restaurants. For the food, you could see the food pictures which gives you a better sense what to eat.

List view

Of course, if you like you could use the list view to quickly browse recommended dish pictures or dish pictures of restaurants.

Restaurant detail

By entering restaurant page, you could see picture, ingredient and price of every dish.

Dish detail

The dish page shows you every detail you need to know about this dish. Also you could add this dish to favourite or rate it.

SO, WHY SHOULD YOU USE MENU?

Imaging this, you, a hungry tourist, are standing on a street in an unfamiliar city, try to find some nice food.

You take out your phone, open Yelp, brower restaurants nearby. They all look nice, 4 stars, lots of recommendations. It is hard to choose one. So you click on one restaurant, want to see some pictures of the dishes. But, wait, what?! Just one picture?!

Yelp does not always help because there is no dish pictures and recommendations.

That is why you should use MENU, see the dish pictures!

Ok then, you click another one. This one seems great, reasonable price, not too far. And more important, the food pictures posted by other users look really delicious. So that is it.
