Find nice food, for you!

WHAT IS MENU

Menu is to help you find great local food! We only focus on food. You could find your favourite food by pictures, rates and comments. And furthermore, menu could recommend food based on your personal preference.

Browsing nearby food & restaurants in map view

Once you open menu, you could browse nearby food and restaurants. For the food, you could see the food pictures which gives you a better sense of what are they.

List view

Of course, you could also use the list view to quickly browse recommended dish pictures or dish pictures of restaurants.

Restaurant detail

By entering a restaurant page, you could see food pictures, ingredients and prices.

Dish detail

The dish page shows you every detail you need to know about this dish. Also you could save this dish to your favourite, rate it or comment.

SO, WHY SHOULD YOU USE MENU?

Imaging this, you, a hungry tourist, are standing on a street in an unfamiliar city, try to find some nice food.

You take out your phone, open Yelp, brower restaurants nearby. They all look nice, 4 stars, lots of recommendations. It is hard to choose one. So you click on one restaurant, want to see some pictures of the dishes. But, wait, what?! No food pictures?!

Yelp does not always help because there is no dish pictures and recommendations.

That is why you should use MENU, see the dish pictures!

Ok then, you click another one. This one seems great, reasonable price, not too far. And more important, the food pictures posted by other users look really delicious. So that is it.

15 minutes later, you are sitting at a table in this restaurant, holding a 5 pages menu, and … get lost, without knowing what these dishes are and which one to order.There are no pictures in this confusing menu, but names, prices and ingredients. You feel helpless, like a innocent child. While a nice, smiling waitress is waiting beside, you do not waste more time, so you decide to take your chance by ordering something seems good. And well, may god bless you.

This menu is really frustrating, because I do not know what all the food are, how they taste and which is best for me.

That is why you should use MENU, see the dish pictures, rates and comments!

DESIGN PROCESS

DEFINE PROBLEM

I am aivd for Yelp. It helps me a lot finding great local restaurant. But as an international student, usually I find myself suffering understanding the restaurants’ menu. What if they could just show me how the dishes looks! So this is where our initial idea of MENU comes from.

But is this a common need that not only for interactional students but also local students? After some interviews, we thought the answer is YES.

BRAINSTORMING

We did brainstorming several times, met every two days and iterated our ideas and sketches. We got many ideas and we killed most of them to keep our design as simple as possible.

ITERATION

We did many iterations. Here are three typical iteration designs of homepage I would like to share with you. They have different focus.

Iteration-1

It emphasises browsing food quickly by showing the food in big pictures without details and the location of the restaurant. The cons would be the users could quickly browse food pictures and make decision.

Iteration-2

The second one adds dish details but may slow the browsing speed. The cons would be that some users who are sensitive of the price or ingredients may find the details very helpful.

Iteration-3

The third one emphasises the restaurant location while with less pictures and no food details. The cons would be users could have a better sense of the location and navigate easily on the map.

TO BE DONE

We know this is a very simplified solution. To be a real product we still need to answer a lot of questions like where does these pictures come from; How to promote users to share their pictures and give comments; How to provide more help for specific needs, and so on.

It is never easy to make a real product. But we believe this is a good beginning.
