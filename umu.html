UMU

Instantly turn audience feedback into revealing data. Make your trainings more insightful and fun!

BACKGROUND

UMU is especially useful for those who want to interact with their audience when giving a speech, a class, a presentation and so on.

I worked closely with the founder, a former senior manager in Google, China, who has more than ten yearsâ€™ experience in training and education.

I completed the information architecture, the first version interaction design of the website and iOS app.

AGILE WORK PROCESS

We employed agile work process and moving forward very fast. We kept everything quick and dirty, met everyday, discussed product requirements, sketching, made prototype, met again, made changes, passed fixed interaction design prototype to visual designer and then moved to next one.

WEB DESIGN

The website is for teachers to manage their events including adding, editing, making schedule and so on.

Event Schedule

Event schedule is the core page where users could check their schedule, add event and edit existing events.

Event Editing

A event contains several sessions. There are six types of session templates. Users could add sessions based on their needs.

MOBILE DESIGN

This is the first version of UMU app design for iOS. Because of the time constrain, we implemented the same design for Android. But we will design for Android later.

Information Architecture

This is a very brief structure demonstrating information hierarchy. I tried to make the structure as flat as possible, no more than four levels.

Interaction Flows and Prototypes

Interaction flows of four tabs and some hi-fidelity prototypes.

AFTERWORDS
