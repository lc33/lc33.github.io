UMU

UMU is a multiplatform interactive system that lets you engage and interacte with your audience. You could set the questions, votes, discussions and other interactive sessions. Your audience could give their feedback though their phones.

Background

UMU is especially useful for those who want to interact with their audience when giving a speeh, a course, a presentation and so on.
I worked as a freelancer for this start-up. Between June to July 2014, I completed the information architecture and most web pages interaction design. During January to February 2015, I finished the first version of UMU iOS interaction design.

Agile Work Process

Being agile, we work very effectively. I work with the founder directly. He has ten years experience in this field, so the product requirements are clear and accureate. And because he gives classes all the time around the whole country, whenever we updated our system, we could test it during his class and get realtime feedback. It helps a lot for us iterating our design.

Web Prototypes

This is a complicated system with many details. We are not hoping to finish everything in one version. We first reviewed all the product requirements and then prioritized them. Divided them into sub-missions based on week. Next, we started from the most prioritisd modules. Here I would like to demonstrate some early prototypes. The project is till going, I will update it from time to time.

Course Schedule

Designed by Axure

Mobile Prptotypes

We started to work on UMU iOS App design after we released the website. Mobile platform will be our main focus in future. We expect our users would performance all tasks on their phones which means this App basically has every critical functions that the website has.
