UMU

UMU is a multiplatform interactive system that lets you engage and interacti with your audience. You could set the questions, votes, discussions and other interactive sessions. Your audience could give their feedback though their phones.

visit umu.com

Background

UMU is especially useful for those who want to interact with their audience when giving a speeh, a course, a presentation and so on. I work as a freelancer for this start-up to design their website and mobile app.

Agile Work Process

Being agile, we work very effectively. I work with the founder directly. He has ten years experience in this field, so product requirements he brought are clear and accureate. And because he gives speeches all the time, so whenever we updated our system, we could test and get useful feedback. It helps a lot when we iterate our design.
