WHAT IS UMU

UMU is especially useful for those who want to interact with their audience when giving a speech, a class, a presentation and so on.

HOW DOES IT WORK

This is an example shows how Dennis, an IT trainer, uses UMU to help him interact with his audience.

Dennis Park
25
IT trainer
San Francisco

Dennis will give a training to 25 people about how to use UMU on Mar. 21 at San Francisco.

Step 1 - Add a new event

Before the training, Dennis logins UMU to add a new event on Mar. 21.

In the video shown right, we could see he added a pre-work survey to know better about his audience.

STEP 2 - INVITE audience TO ANSWER THE SURVEY

On Mar. 21, Dennis started the training with this pre-work survey. He used a projector to show the audience how to access the survey with their phones.

Opne browser - Go to umu.com - enter "216327" - click "Join Session" - Ready to go

STEP 3 - THE audience ANSWER THE SURVEY USING THEIR PHONES

Followed the instruction, the audience answered the survery on their phones.

STEP 4 - Visualize survey results

After all the audience finished the survey, the results are visualized on the projector screen.

By using UMU, Dennis could easily interact with his audience, get real time interactive visual feedbacks. And even more, with all of the data collected and analyzed, UMU could help Dennis know better his audience and improve his skills.

DESIGN PROCESS

AGILE, AGILE AND AGILE

We employed agile work process and moving forward very fast. We kept everything quick and dirty, met everyday, discussed product requirements, sketching, made prototype, met again, made changes, passed fixed interaction design prototype to visual designer and then moved to next one.
