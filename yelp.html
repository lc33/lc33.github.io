ABOUT YELP E-COMMERCE CHALLENGE

Create an ecommerce product detail and/or search page for a physical product of your choice. Each product has a title, multiple photos, a description, a rating, multiple reviews, user uploaded photos, a price, shipping information, stock information and a buy or add to cart button. You are encouraged to add additional elements.

You can create your own branding or use Yelp’s branding and design as if it’s a complementary product. Optimize your design towards get users into the purchase funnel.
