WHAT IS USVERSITY

"USVERSITY" is a combination of "US" + "UNIVERSITY". I made up this word trying to convey the idea that this project is to build connections between us and universities.

This is my capstone project. I designed for prospective students to give them an enhanced experience with VR tech to know better their target universities.
